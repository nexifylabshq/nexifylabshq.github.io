Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the AIPDFEditor app developed by Nexify Labs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our services:

  • Google account name and email (when you sign in with Google)
  • Phone number (when you sign in with phone OTP)
  • PDF files you choose to process (processed locally on device)
  • App usage data (via Firebase Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our app features
  • To sync your documents via Google Drive (only with your permission)
  • To send OTP verification codes
  • To show relevant ads to guest users (via Google AdMob)

3. PDF Processing

All PDF processing happens locally on your device. Your PDF files are never uploaded to our servers. When you use AI features, PDF content is sent directly to Google Gemini API — we do not store this data.

4. Google Drive Backup

Cloud backup is optional. When enabled, your PDFs are stored in your own Google Drive account. We never access your Drive data without permission.

5. Advertising

Guest users may see ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to show personalized ads. You can opt out in your device settings under Google → Ads.

6. Third Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Authentication)
  • Google Gemini API (AI features)
  • Google AdMob (Advertisements)
  • Google Drive API (Cloud backup)

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures. All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS. We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data anytime
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of analytics
  • Disable cloud backup anytime

11. Changes to This Policy
